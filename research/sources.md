## Evidence rules

- Prefer primary specifications, product documentation, and direct observed
  behavior.
- A competitor source supports only a statement about that competitor.
- A protocol precedent does not prove product demand.
- Community wikis are useful primary descriptions of community practice but
  are not independent market evidence.
- No source in this register supports a total-addressable-market estimate.
