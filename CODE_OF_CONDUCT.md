# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We pledge to make participation in Card Commons a harassment-free experience
for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, sex
characteristics, gender identity and expression, level of experience,
education, socioeconomic status, nationality, personal appearance, race,
religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Our standards

Positive behavior includes empathy, constructive feedback, respect for
different experiences, and graceful acceptance of responsibility. Unacceptable
behavior includes harassment, discrimination, intimidation, sexualized
attention, deliberate disruption, and publishing another person's private
information.

## Enforcement

Report conduct concerns privately through the security contact described in
[SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md). Maintainers may remove, edit, or reject
contributions and may temporarily or permanently restrict participation.

This policy adapts the Contributor Covenant, version 2.1.
